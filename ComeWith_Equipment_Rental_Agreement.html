1 — Renter responsibilities
Renter agrees to use all equipment safely and for its intended purpose. Renter assumes full financial liability for any damage, theft, loss, or destruction during the rental period. Equipment must be returned in the same condition as received. Normal wear does not include scratches, dents, liquid damage, or functional impairment.
2 — Late returns
Equipment not returned by the agreed deadline will incur a late fee of $ per day. Come With reserves the right to report unreturned equipment as theft after days past the return deadline.
3 — Damage & liability
In the event of damage, Renter is responsible for the full cost of repair or replacement at current market value. Come With is not responsible for injury, loss, or damage to third parties. Renter agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Come With from any claims arising from Renter's use of the equipment.
4 — Cancellation
Cancellations with fewer than 48 hours notice may forfeit any deposit paid. Cancellations with 48+ hours notice are eligible for a full refund of any deposit.

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of New York County.
