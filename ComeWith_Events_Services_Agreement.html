Terms & conditions
1 — Cancellation policy
Cancellation with 30+ days notice: deposit fully refundable. 14–29 days: 50% of deposit refundable. Fewer than 14 days: deposit non-refundable. Day of event: full fee is due. If Come With must cancel, all payments received will be refunded in full.
2 — Performance standards
Come With will arrive no less than hours before set start time. Extensions beyond the contracted set require mutual agreement and may incur additional charges at $ per hour.
3 — Force majeure
Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including natural disasters, government mandates, or venue closure. Parties will negotiate in good faith regarding rescheduling or refund.
4 — Indemnification
Come With's total liability shall not exceed total fees paid. Client agrees to indemnify Come With from claims arising from the Client's event, venue conditions, or acts of attendees.
5 — Governing law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of New York County.
